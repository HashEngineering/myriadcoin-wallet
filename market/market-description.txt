Have your Myriadcoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Myriadcoin Wallet is the first mobile Myriadcoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Myriadcoin amount in MYR, mMYR and µMYR.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Myriadcoin via NFC, QR-codes or Myriadcoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Myriadcoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Myriadcoin balance.

You can find more about Myriadcoin at
http://myriad.theblockexplorer.com
